Some Beautiful Calculus

The Fundamental Theorem of Calculus:

\[\int_a^b f(x)\,dx = F(b) - F(a)\]

Derivative of e^x:

\[\frac{d}{dx}e^x = e^x\]

Integration by Parts:

\[\int u\,dv = uv - \int v\,du\]

Taylor Series:

\[f(x) = \sum_{n=0}^{\infty} \frac{f^{(n)}(a)}{n!}(x-a)^n\]
